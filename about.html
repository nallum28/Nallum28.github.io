About Me

Hi there! I'm a Cyber Security and Computer Networks enthusiast, driven by a passion for understanding the digital world and solving its most complex challenges. From my time at Glasgow Caledonian University to volunteering in various roles, I've built a strong foundation in tech and community service, and I'm ready to take the next step toward a career in cybersecurity.

My Journey

I first discovered my interest in Cyber Security when I was exploring the intersection of technology and problem-solving. Studying Cyber Security at Glasgow Caledonian University allowed me to delve deep into network defenses, ethical hacking, and digital forensics, building not only technical skills but also a passion for helping others understand the importance of protecting their online space.

But learning doesn't stop in the classroom! I've had the chance to apply my knowledge and grow my skill set through various roles:

  • Student Outreach Mentor - Guided and mentored fellow students, helping them navigate university life while fostering a sense of community.
  • Football Steward - A role that taught me responsibility and working under pressure, ensuring safety at large events.
  • Ethical Hacking Treasurer - Managed the finances of the ethical hacking club, supporting events that brought hackers and security enthusiasts together.
  • Cyber Clinic Volunteer - Worked closely with local businesses to improve their cybersecurity posture, offering free advice and solutions to real-world problems.
